Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, a rich and creamy Indian dish, is a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. The succulent chicken, marinated in a blend of aromatic spices and yogurt, is then cooked to perfection in a velvety smooth tomato-based sauce. This indulgent dish is a true culinary masterpiece that will leave you craving for more.

Whether you're a seasoned cook or a kitchen novice, this recipe is easy to follow and yields restaurant-quality results. So, gather your ingredients, roll up your sleeves, and get ready to embark on a flavor-filled journey that will transport you to the vibrant streets of India.

Ingredients

For the Marinade :
  • 1 kg boneless, skinless chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

For the Sauce:
  • Butter
  • Onion, ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Heavy cream
  • Cream Cheese and fresh coriander
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Combine chicken with yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and salt. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Fry the marinated chicken until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. Heat butter and oil in a pot. Saut√© onion, ginger-garlic paste, and spices until fragrant. Add crushed tomatoes and cook until the sauce thickens
  4. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce. Stir in heavy cream, butter, and cream cheese. Simmer until well combined. Garnish with fresh coriander.
