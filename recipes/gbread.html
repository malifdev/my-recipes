Garlic Bread

Garlic bread is a timeless classic that pairs perfectly with a variety of dishes, from hearty soups to juicy steaks. It's simplicity belies its flavor-packed punch. A few key ingredients—fresh garlic, butter, and herbs transform ordinary bread into an irresistible side dish or appetizer.

In this recipe, we'll guide you through the process of creating the perfect garlic bread, from selecting the right loaf to achieving that golden-brown, buttery crust.Whether you're a seasoned home cook or a novice in the kitchen, this recipe is easy to follow and delivers restaurant-quality results.

Ingredients :

  • 1 loaf of Italian or French bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper

Steps :

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine softened butter, minced garlic, Parmesan cheese, parsley, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Cut the bread loaf in half lengthwise.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture generously over both cut sides of the bread.
  5. Place the bread on a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly.
  6. Cut into slices and serve immediately.
